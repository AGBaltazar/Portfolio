Where I've Been

A technical sponge coming from a small town trying to make BIG impact! Growing up in a rural town meant I had little to no access to home wifi; but with a cell phone and Google this was quickly a thing of the past. This was the beginning of my journey into technology. Now growing up rooting phones, replacing screens, or passing Core 1 of CompTia A+ exam (Their are 2 Tests/Cores) most of my youth was spent in tech. Once I hit college is when I began to program, starting with Python I have had exposure to C++, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, Calculus (hepled my analytical skills), and even Object-Oriented Programming.

What I Hope To Do

Like I said; I'm trying to make a BIG impact! I want to give back to where I grew up, I want to teach what was not available, and I want to help succeed those that come after me. Growing up in a small town with little to no internet meant I had the world to explore, but no access to certain careers such as technology. I want to create programs to help kids that come from underserved towns and give them an opportunity to learn a new skill!
